Name:  Chicken Marsala
Author: Lisa
Description: A delicious, classic chicken dish -- lightly coated chicken breasts braised with Marsala wine and mushrooms. Easy and ideal for both a quick weeknight entree AND serving to company.
Time: 30 m
Servings: 4
Calories: 448

Ingredients:
0.25 cup flour
0.5 teaspoon salt
2 dash black pepper
0.5 teaspoon oregano
4 - chicken breast
4 tablespoon butter
4 tablespoon olive oil
1 cup mushrooms
0.5 cup Marsala wine
0.25 cup sherry

Directions:
In a shallow dish or bowl, mix together the flour, salt, pepper and oregano. Coat chicken pieces in flour mixture.
In a large skillet, melt butter in oil over medium heat. Place chicken in the pan, and lightly brown. Turn over chicken pieces, and add mushrooms. Pour in wine and sherry. Cover skillet; simmer chicken 10 minutes, turning once, until no longer pink and juices run clear.
