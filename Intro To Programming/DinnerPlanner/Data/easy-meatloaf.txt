Name: Easy Meatloaf
Author: Janet Caldwell
Description: This is a very easy and no fail recipe for meatloaf. It won't take long to make at all, and it's quite good!
Time: 1 h 10 m
Servings: 8
Calories: 372

Ingredients:
1.5 pound ground beef
1 - egg
1 - onion
1 cup milk
1 cup bread crumbs
2 tablespoon brown sugar
2 tablespoon prepared mustard
0.33 cup ketchup

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
In a large bowl, combine the beef, egg, onion, milk and bread OR cracker crumbs. Season with salt and pepper to taste and place in a lightly greased 5x9 inch loaf pan, OR form into a loaf and place in a lightly greased 9x13 inch baking dish.
In a separate small bowl, combine the brown sugar, mustard and ketchup. Mix well and pour over the meatloaf.
Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 1 hour.
